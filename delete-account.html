Delete Your GoalStake Social Account

Users may request deletion of their GoalStake Social account at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to:

support@goalstake.net

Use the subject line:

Delete My GoalStake Social Account

Data Deleted

  • User account information
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Predictions
  • League memberships
  • Leaderboard participation records

Data Retention

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Backup records may be retained for legal, security, and fraud-prevention purposes for up to 90 days.

Contact

support@goalstake.net
